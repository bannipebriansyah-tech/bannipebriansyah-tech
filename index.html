Whistleblowing System

The Whistleblowing System (WisPU) is a platform provided by the Ministry of Public Works and Housing (Kementerian PUPR) of Indonesia for the public and civil servants (ASN - Aparatur Sipil Negara) to report suspected violations occurring within the ministry. This system ensures that whistleblowers can submit information about misconduct, ethical violations, or breaches of regulations while maintaining confidentiality.

BINUS School

These schools are located in various cities across Indonesia, including Jakarta (Simprug), South Tangerang (Serpong), Bekasi, Semarang, and Surabaya.
